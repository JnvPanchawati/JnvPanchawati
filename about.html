Our Facilities

At Jawahar navodaya vidyalaya, we take pride in providing a well-rounded environment that supports the academic, physical, and personal growth of our students. Our campus is equipped with modern facilities designed to enhance learning and student life. Some of the key facilities include:

library

The school library is a quiet and resource-rich space designed to encourage reading and research. It is stocked with a wide range of books, magazines to support the learning needs of students across all grades. Our library is an excellent place for students to explore new ideas, study, and engage with a wealth of educational materials.

Play Ground

Our spacious playground is a safe and enjoyable space for students to engage in physical activities. With well-maintained sports fields and play equipment, it provides opportunities for outdoor sports, games, and recreational activities. Physical fitness and teamwork are key aspects of student life at Jawahar navodaya vidyalaya, and the playground offers a perfect space for students to unwind and stay active.

Canteen

Our school canteen offers a variety of healthy and nutritious meals, providing students with balanced options during their break times. The canteen is designed to offer a welcoming atmosphere where students can relax and enjoy their meals.
