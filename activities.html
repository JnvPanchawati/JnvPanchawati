Police Department, Billi Ground on 18.10.2024

On 18.10.2024. the officers from the police department, Billi ground conducted i career guidance program at JNV Panchawati. The initiative aimed to provide students with insights into career options, requirements for different career paths, and the importance of responsible citizenship.

Objective

The primary objective of this program was to guide students in making informed career choices. Police officers aimed to inspire students to set goals and offered valuable advice on career planning, the importance of discipline, and the dedication required for various career fields.

Conclusion

The program was a success, fostering a positive relationship between students and law enforcement. The Police Departments plans to continue such outreach programs to guide and mentor young students in making wise career choices. This career guidance initiative by the police department has set a foundation for future collaborations between schools and local agencies, aiming to benefit students ni their academic and career pursuits.
